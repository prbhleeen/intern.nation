Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to intern.nation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Internship and Placement Management Portal.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, contact number, and resume/CV.
  • Academic information such as college, course, grades, and certifications.
  • Application history, job preferences, and communications within the platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect students with potential employers and internship providers.
  • To improve platform features and personalize your experience.
  • To send updates, notifications, and relevant job alerts.

4. Data Sharing

We only share your information with verified companies and recruiters for internship/placement purposes. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting our support team.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via email or platform notifications.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@intern.nation.
